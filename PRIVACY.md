# Privacy Policy for WhatsApp Automation Pro

This app provides a connection between your ERPNext instance and a third-party WhatsApp SaaS (WhatsJet). 

### Data Handling
1. **API Keys**: All API tokens and credentials are stored securely within your Frappe/ERPNext database using standard encryption.
2. **Message Logs**: This app does not store the content of your WhatsApp messages on external servers. It only facilitates the transfer of data from your ERPNext instance to your specified API endpoint.
3. **Data Sharing**: No data is shared with NexGen Enterprises. All communications happen directly between your server and your WhatsApp instance.

### User Responsibility
Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of WhatsApp for business communication complies with WhatsApp’s Terms of Service and local data privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
